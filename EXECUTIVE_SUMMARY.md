# Executive Summary — Amman Digital Market Analytics

## Overview
This analysis reviewed sales data from Amman Digital Market covering around 500 orders between January 2024 and June 2025. After removing cancelled orders and obvious data errors, we examined revenue trends, customer behavior by city, and product performance to help guide business decisions.

## Top Findings

1. **Books has become our strongest category**, generating the highest revenue among all product types.  
   This category clearly outperforms the others. (See: 02_revenue_by_category.png)

2. **The business delivered very strong growth last month**, with revenue jumping by more than 151% compared to the previous month.  
   This is a significant acceleration in sales. (See: 01_multi_panel_kpis.png)

3. **Amman continues to generate the largest share of total revenue**, but customers in other cities spend roughly the same amount per order as those in Amman.  
   (See: 04_revenue_by_city.png)

## Recommendations

1. **Focus more effort on the Books category**: Since Books is our top performer, we should expand the range of books available, run special promotions, and consider bundling them with other products to increase overall order value.

2. **Build on the recent strong growth**: The 151% increase last month is excellent news. We should quickly understand what drove this jump (marketing, new products, or seasonal demand) and repeat what worked to keep the momentum going.

3. **Expand marketing outside Amman**: Although Amman brings the most revenue, customers elsewhere spend similarly per order. Increasing targeted campaigns in Irbid, Zarqa, and Aqaba will help us grow the customer base and reduce our heavy dependence on the capital.
